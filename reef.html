The Reef of JMJ

Not strong flavored, and steak like texture. Sailfish meat has a strong red tinge, and spots of darker flesh running along the line of the backbone. Its flavor is variable between fish, sometimes mild and sometimes strong. The fillets are relatively large, so you’ll see it primarily in the form of “loins,” or sliced into steaks.
