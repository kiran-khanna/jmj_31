23 Years of Seafood Kingdom

JMJ Seafoods Private Limited an off-spring of JMJ Seafoods Pvt Ltd a 2 decade old processor and exporter of high quality seafood from India. Specialising in Yellow Fin Tuna & Sword Fish as our primary products - JMJ has remained a major player in the industry. JMJ believes in innovation; and innovation leads to change; JMJ is now innovating with its range of seafood by value adding and creating healthy eating. At JMJ all products are processed under the most stringent international standards across approved and certified processing facilities backed by international certifications - dealing strictly with sustainable seafood. 20 years of commitment, 20 years of riding the tide, 20 years where everyday remains a starting point. Our constant will to improve and innovate has led us to become a leader on national and overseas markets; with a constant aim to offer our customers with the highest possible seafood and seafood innovation.
